Help Center

Orders:

Can i update my address?

Only the original person who place the order can update their address. As long as the order has not shipped, we can update your andress. Please send us an e-mail(from the e-mail address associated with the order) with the order name and the correct address to which you are lokking to ship. If the order has shipped, we cannot update your address.

Can I cancel my order?

If your item hasn't shipped, send a cancellation request to Hio Support: help@hiopipes.com and a team member wil assist you. If your order has shipped, we cannot cancel the order but will help you create a return. Certain types of items cannot be returned, such as custom products special orders or personalized items

Shipping & Returns:

What are my shipping opions?

We are engaged in the wholesale of glass pipes. To make the price more competitive and meet the needs of small orders, we usually only use free shipping.

When will my items ship?

Our products are made to order and do not exist in stock, usually we need 5-10 days to produce these products. After the order arrives in your country by international flight, it will be delivered by the local courier, PLEASE NOTE: We cannot guarantee the arrival date or time (usually within 15-25 days) after the goods are handed over to she shipping company. While we do our best to prevent any delays, order may be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances. Compared with the exquisite design, favorable price, and high-end quality of the product, we think it is very worthile, once you have long-term cooperation with us, please arrange the purchansing time of the order.

Where is my tracking number?

When the order is created, the salesperson will send the first email to inform the bank account information. After receiving the payment from the customer, we will arrange the production and packaging. When the order is shipped, this time we will send a second email with the tracking number. You can track the shipment on the website we give based on the tracking number. Most tracking numbers will not update within the first 24 hours.

How are my items shipping? Will people know I just bought a cannabis accessory

Hio Pieces come in a custom box with built-in foam or paper-purp trays. We ship all of our products in unassuming brown boxes, this will protect the privacy of our customers well.

What happens if my items break during shipping?

In the unlikely event you receive a damaged or defective item, Hio Pipes will replace it at no cost. Notify the Hio Support team within 48 hours of delivery with an order number and a few photos of the packaging and damaged item(e). We'll take care of the rest. *Please don't throw anything away until you speak with a Hio Support team member. We might need the package back! We always provide a prepaid return shipping label if you're asked to ship a package.

Do you offer free shipping on returns?

We do not offer free return shipping at this time Hio Support will provide you with a shipping label, once we receive the return and confirm the state of the product, Hio Pipes will issue a refund less the original shipping amount.

How long do I have to make a return?

Return must be requested within one week of receiving the product. Once a case is approved, the order must be sent back within 15 days of approval. All cases order than 15 days that have not been sent in or given further communication will be canceled and archived, on longer eligible for return or exchange.

How do I ensure I get a full refund?

only unused items in an undamaged box will be issued a refund. Once your returned item(e) are received and inspected, we will process the requested exchange, return. Items returned for a refund will be assessed, and if needed receive a flat $5 repackaging fee per item. If no repackaging is necessary, the fee will be waived.
